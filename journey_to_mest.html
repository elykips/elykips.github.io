My journey to Mest

Several weeks of personal reflection had elapsed after final interviews with the MEST Entrepreneurs In Training program for 2018 and I was becoming a worried man. Reasons: the suspense was killing me from the inside. I so badly needed to know whether or not i was going to have a date with greatness at MEST. Every minute, i found myself refreshing my mails anticipating for an email from Amma Bafoe, the MEST Recruitment and Marketing Lead. A couple of days later, in one of my boring afternoon, i received an email alert on my phone but was hesitant to check it out since i had issues with my boss that morning after skipping a team building meeting. An email that could break or make me, "MEST Application Final Feedback" hit my face when i finally got to my mails. I held my breath and tried to compose myself in order to come to terms with the outcome. I had made it to the Meltwter Entrepreneurial School of Technology, EIT 2018 program! I was so excited and could hardly conceal my happiness. A ray of hope finally rekindled after missing the program in the previous year. I got determined to do my best in the program now that i had gotten a one-in-a-lifetime opportunity to tremendously change my career life.
